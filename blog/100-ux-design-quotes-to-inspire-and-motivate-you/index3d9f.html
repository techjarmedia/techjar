100 UX Design Quotes to Inspire and Motivate You

Written by Kristina Guzikova | Sep 21, 2016 2:00:44 PM

Good design considers users; how they feel, how they think, and how they make decisions. Enter: User Experience (UX) Design. UX Design is vital for creating meaningful, impactful user experiences that turn users into lifelong customers. Still not convinced? We’ve put together a compilation of 100 inspiring, tweetable quotes from some of our favorite influencers and experts in the UX community- even including some from before the term “UX” had been coined.
