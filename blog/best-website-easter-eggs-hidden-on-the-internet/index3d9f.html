Best Website Easter Eggs Hidden on the Internet

Written by Sloane Moore | Apr 11, 2017 2:26:28 PM

It’s finally spring, which means that Easter is just around the corner. While others may be busy dying Easter eggs, being a web development agency, we’re after Easter eggs of a different sort. You know, the website kind! For those of you who don’t know, this type of Easter egg is a hidden feature of a piece of media (generally a website, computer program, or video game). Here’s a collection of the best website Easter eggs for your viewing delight. Quick tip: Some of these Easter eggs require the “Konami Code.” Find these features by typing this code using the arrows and keyboard: “Up, Up, Down, Down, Down, Left, Right, Left, Right, B, A.” Happy hunting!

Hint: Do a Google image search for “Atari breakout.”

Hint: Do the Konami code. Get ready for a tasty snack.

Hint: Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the scissors.

5. Dance Sport Info

Hint: Do the Konami code. Time for some old school fun!

6. Wikipedia - Easter Egg Entry

Hint: Click on the little guy in the bottom right of the picture.

7. Coca-Cola Company Website

Hint: Right click on the page and select "View the source code."

8. Google

Hint: Type “do a barrel roll” in Google search. Whee!

9. Eren Emre 

Hint: Let's do another barrel roll. Do the Konami code for this one.

10. YouTube

Hint: Type in “do the Harlem shake” and make sure your speakers are on!

11. Teddy-o-Ted

Do the Konami code. Get some popcorn and relax.

12. Elliott Kember
