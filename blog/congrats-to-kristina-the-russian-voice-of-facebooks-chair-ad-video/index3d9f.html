Congrats to Kristina - the Russian Voice of Facebook's Chair Ad Video

Written by Danielle Kogan | Oct 16, 2012 9:13:11 PM

One of Intechnic’s talented team members, Kristina Guzikova, recently offered her amazing voice over skills to help create the Russian version of Facebook’s recent Chair Video ad.  If you haven’t seen it yet, head over to Facebook’s homepage to check it out, or watch the Chair ad with Kristina’s voice over in Russian below.

Kristina is also an experienced creative project manager, and is currently focusing on digital media production.

“I love to act, either in front of the camera or behind the microphone.  While creating, I’m able to transform the space around me into an extra-ordinary playground and give every project a unique twist.” - Kristina Guzikova -  

Congrats to Kristina on her voice over project!
